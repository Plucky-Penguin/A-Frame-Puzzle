THE TOWER OF TERROR

Greetings, you are a famous treasure hunter that has discovered an ancient tower containing untold riches.

Your intel says that all of the treasure is at the top of the tower. Thankfully, someone kindly setup ladders

for you to get to the top. However, you are too cautious to use them without the proper support. In order to

make sure the ladders are safe, complete the puzzles to unlock the safety rails. To climb the ladder, look

through the bars at the red safety rails. You will know if you got a question wrong, because of an X that appears.

You are only punished for wrong answers on the final question. However, if you get that question wrong, the tower

will use it's ancient power to banish you to THE VOID, where you will roam for eternity.

Good Luck.
